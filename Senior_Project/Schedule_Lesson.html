Hitting

Stephen has always taught his batters to look to drive the ball up the middle.

By doing this, it shortens the swing and makes the batter stay inside of the ball.

Another important thing is to get the bat on the plane of the pitch, and try to stay on that plane for as long as you can.

This resulted in the barrel of the bat staying in the zone for longer, and making it less likely to be fooled by an off-speed pitch.
